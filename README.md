# 📊 Supply Chain & Business Insights Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔗 Project Overview  
This project presents a comprehensive **Business Intelligence dashboard** built using Power BI, SQL, and Excel.  

It provides a **360° view of business performance** across:
- Executive Decision Making  
- Finance  
- Sales & Marketing  
- Product & Inventory  
- Supply Chain  

The dashboard is designed from a **CEO perspective**, focusing on profitability, operational efficiency, and strategic insights.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights  

- 🚚 Logistics cost (~$37M) significantly impacts overall profitability  
- ⏱️ ~25% deliveries are delayed → operational inefficiency  
- 🏭 Warehouse performance is uneven → distribution imbalance  
- 📉 Revenue shows decline trend after peak → growth concern  
- 🌍 Heavy dependency on one region (North) → business risk  
- 📦 High inventory turnover indicates efficiency but potential stock risk  
- 🔄 Supplier lead time varies widely (17–27 days) → inconsistency  

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations  

- Optimize logistics using **multi-modal transport strategies**  
- Improve delivery performance with **supplier SLAs & monitoring**  
- Implement **demand forecasting models**  
- Balance warehouse load through **inventory redistribution**  
- Expand in underperforming regions to reduce dependency
